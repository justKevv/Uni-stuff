INFORMATICS ENGINEERING

In 2010, based on the needs of the community and the industry related to the Diploma IV (D-IV) program in the field of informatics, Polinema established a new study program, Diploma IV (D-IV) in Informatics Engineering (IE). At the beginning of its establishment, the number of students in the D-IV IE program consisted of only 46 students, but by 2015, the number of students in the D-IV IE program had increased to 545 students.

The rapid growth in the number of students in all D-III IE and D-IV IE study programs prompted the leadership at Polinema to merge these two study programs under one roof under the Department of Electrical Engineering. Based on this, in 2015, under Director's Decree Number 53, in order to improve the quality of management and optimize resources, the Department of Information Technology (JTI) was established with programs in D-III IE and D-IV IE.
